🧠 Optimize Self : Fix Yourself First

You can't help others if you're a mess:
  • Keep your word: Do what you say you'll do
  • Know your triggers: Understand what sets you off
  • Manage your time: Don't be the bottleneck
  • Admit your mistakes: Own it when you screw up
  • Keep learning: Stay curious about new stuff
  • Take care of yourself: You can't run on empty forever
Think of it like putting on your own oxygen mask first. You help nobody if you're passing out.
